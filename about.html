About

Building companies that last.

We are on a mission to build companies that can be handed down to the generations after us.

Definition

Traditio is a Latin word meaning "to hand over" or "to pass down."

That idea sits at the center of the company. We want to build businesses that are worth keeping and worth handing down.

For us, that means patience, clear standards, and a long-term commitment to building things well.

How We Think

Build well. Operate well. Keep what matters.

Long-Term

We build and invest in businesses for the long-term. Generational impact is more important to us than quick cash.

Disciplined

Good companies are usually built through long and steady execution.

Multigenerational

We want to build businesses that will be handed down for generations.
